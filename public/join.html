Questions

Frequently Asked

Why don't I get access immediately after paying?
Everyone in a cohort starts together on the 8th. This means your accountability partner starts on the same day, the community is in sync, and the live sessions align with your progress. It's not a delay — it's how the challenge is designed to work.
What happens on the 8th?
You'll receive an email with a link that logs you straight into the app. Your account is created, Day 1 begins, and you complete your first Morning Check-In. That's it.
What currency is the payment in?
The challenge is priced in Botswana Pula (P350). PayPal will convert to your card's currency at the current exchange rate.
What if I miss a day?
Your streak resets. You get a warning. Three strikes and the challenge restarts from Day 1. The accountability is real — that's the point.
Is there a refund policy?
No refunds after access is granted on the 8th. If you have a genuine emergency before access, contact us directly.
Who is this for?
People who are serious. People who are tired of starting and stopping. People who need a system, a community, and someone to answer to. If you're looking for something easy, this is not it.
